What is 3D Grimoire?

By: Matthew Greenlaw

October 31

Magicians have used a tome called a "Grimoire" (grÍm’wä(r)’) to document how they cast their spells and make their magical items for ages. Magicians passed these grimoires along to their successors and used them to share practices with their cohorts. 3D printing is kind of like magic; you can take an object, alter it in any way you want, and quickly create a new version. You can even send an object to your cohort who can adjust it to their liking and make their own copies. 3D printer operators have to remember a lot about their projects so it is helpful to write down best practices and material characteristics to use on other projects. 3D Grimoire is your "tomb of magic".


3D Grimoire is designed to be a friendly platform for both beginners and experts by providing the latest news and information, best practices, solutions to common problems, and a strong community involvement.
